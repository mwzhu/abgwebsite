About

The Crypto.Chicks is the 2nd generation of the Crypto.Chick 1/1 which was the first female avatar collection published on Opensea in May 2021. The Crypto.Chicks Collection was created to highlight the importance of women in the crypto space and reflects the individuality and uniqueness of every woman.

The collection is based on the Ethereum blockchain and includes 10k unique gorgeous girls. Every girl has various rare attributes and features, some of them occurring only once!

Exclusive CC Merch

Launching a Branded CC Merch. Part of the profit will be donated to charity.

Q4 2021 Milestones

Community Voting System:an off-chain voting system that accounts for the number of Chicks held by the connected wallet.
Charity Donation:a donation of 5 ETH to women’s rights or women’s empowerment organizations. Nominations by the community and community will vote to select which charity we donate to.
Women NFT Artist Spotlight Series:a monthly blog that will feature women NFT artists from around the world with interviews about their stories and their work. There will be benefits for CC community members included, like whitelist access, giveaways, etc.
Community Event Series:a regular series of fun or educational community events with chances to win prizes and other fun giveaways.
Personalized Merch:an upgraded version of our merch store that will allow you to get merch with your own Chick personalized on it.
NFTs 101 Series:an educational training series to help new entrants to crypto and NFTs understand how to get started and navigate the world of NFTs.

Q1 2022 Milestones

Staking:more details to come, there will be votes using the new community voting system.
Digital Wearables:digital wearables for the Chicks in the metaverse.

Strategic Objectives

Corporate Partnerships:We aim to bridge the gap between the NFT world and major companies by collaborating with major retail, fashion, cosmetic, and toy brands. We have major conversations underway, which we are excited to share with all of you when we’re able to.
Partnerships with other Women-Oriented Projects:We want to dispel the belief that there are limited seats for women-oriented projects at the table and show we can be stronger together with collaborations and partnerships.
Offline events and meetups:We want to build Crypto Chicks into a strong global network, and will facilitate this by hosting global meetups and events to bring the community together.
